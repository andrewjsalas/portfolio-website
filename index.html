About

My name is Andrew J Salas, a Front End Developer and former Sound Engineer from Los Angeles, CA. For the past 7 years I worked as a Sound Engineer and a Mixing and Mastering Engineer at Universal Studios sub-contracted through Diablo Sound.

I began attending College of the Canyons in August of 2020 in pursuit of an Associates Degree in Computer Science. Since then I have been learning and studying while creating a variety of apps and projects. My goal is to develop immersive apps and websites with a team of developers while continuing to grow and progress.
